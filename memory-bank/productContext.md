# Product Context

## Problem Statement
Open water swimming is a growing sport with events held worldwide, but there's no centralized platform for swimmers to discover events. Information is scattered across various websites, social media, and local swimming club pages, making it difficult for enthusiasts to find suitable events based on their preferences for location, distance, and timing.

## Solution
OWSwims provides a centralized, map-based platform that aggregates open water swimming events from around the world. It allows swimmers to easily discover events that match their preferences and provides event organizers with a platform to promote their events to a targeted audience.

## User Experience Goals

### For Swimmers
- **Discovery**: Easily find open water swimming events based on location, date, and distance preferences
- **Information**: Access comprehensive details about events including race distances, wetsuit requirements, pricing, and location information
- **Planning**: Visualize race tracks on the map and understand travel distances
- **Community**: Read and contribute reviews to help other swimmers make informed decisions
- **Accessibility**: Access the platform in multiple languages and on any device

### For Event Organizers
- **Visibility**: Gain exposure to a targeted audience of open water swimming enthusiasts
- **Promotion**: Showcase event details, race tracks, and unique selling points
- **Feedback**: Receive ratings and reviews to improve future events
- **Analytics**: Understand interest levels and engagement with their events

## Value Proposition
- **For Swimmers**: The most comprehensive and user-friendly platform to discover open water swimming events worldwide
- **For Organizers**: A targeted channel to promote events to an engaged community of open water swimming enthusiasts

## Key Differentiators
1. **Map-Based Interface**: Visual discovery of events on an interactive map
2. **Race Track Visualization**: Ability to see the actual swimming routes
3. **Comprehensive Filtering**: Find events that match specific criteria
4. **Community Reviews**: Make informed decisions based on other swimmers' experiences
5. **Multi-Language Support**: Access the platform in your preferred language

## Success Metrics
- Number of listed events
- User engagement (time spent on site, return visits)
- Event discovery (clicks on event details)
- Review submissions
- Organizer sign-ups
